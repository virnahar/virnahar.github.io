Old vs New tax regime for FY 2024-25

This is a practical summary for salaried taxpayers. Rules may change in future budgets, so always validate with current law.

Quick decision logic

  • If your claimable deductions are high, old regime can still win.
  • If deductions are low and salary is straightforward, new regime often wins.
  • Do not guess from salary alone; compare both regimes on real numbers.

Common comparison points

Old vs new regime comparison for salaried taxpayers
Factor Old regime New regime
Deductions (80C, 80D, etc.) Generally available Largely restricted
Standard deduction (salary) Available Available (FY-aware limits apply)
Complexity Higher documentation burden Simpler for many salaried cases

Most common mistake

Choosing a regime before reconciling ITR, AIS, and deductions. The safer approach is to load your actual values in Tax Story and compare old vs new in the same FY.
