Capital gains tax on shares in India: quick basics

This overview is educational and not a substitute for professional tax advice.

Why salaried taxpayers should care

Even if salary is your main income, occasional equity sales can affect return schedules, tax calculation, and mismatch risk if data is not mapped correctly.

Core things to verify

  • Broker contract notes and statement totals.
  • Whether gains are reflected consistently across your records.
  • Any AIS-reported transactions missing from your working sheet.
  • Consistency between tax computation and reported gains.

Practical workflow

Compile gains from your broker statements, align with AIS signals, then validate your final return values. You can use Tax Story to cross-check patterns against your loaded files.
