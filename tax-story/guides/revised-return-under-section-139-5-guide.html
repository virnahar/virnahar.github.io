Revised return under section 139(5): simple guide

Educational overview only. For final legal interpretation, rely on official guidance and your advisor.

When revision is usually considered

  • You discover omitted income or incorrect figures after filing.
  • Key mismatch appears in reconciliation after original filing.
  • You need to correct a material schedule entry.

What to verify before revising

  1. Confirm all corrected amounts with source records.
  2. Recompute regime impact and liability correctly.
  3. Check TDS and key AIS-linked entries again.
  4. Keep a clear note of what changed and why.

Common mistakes in revised returns

  • Changing one schedule but forgetting linked totals.
  • Fixing mismatch without validating source documents.
  • Using old working sheets after new corrections.

Practical workflow
