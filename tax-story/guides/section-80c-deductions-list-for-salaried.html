Section 80C deductions list for salaried taxpayers

This is a practical checklist for common salaried cases. Limits and eligibility can change, so verify current law before final filing.

Common 80C instruments

  • EPF contribution shown in salary records.
  • PPF contributions in the financial year.
  • ELSS mutual fund investments.
  • Life insurance premium (eligible conditions apply).
  • Principal repayment of housing loan (eligible case).
  • Tuition fees (eligible conditions apply).

Frequent mistakes

  • Claiming amounts paid outside the relevant FY.
  • Double counting EPF and other proofs across tools.
  • Assuming every insurance payment is fully eligible.
  • Ignoring regime implications before claiming.
