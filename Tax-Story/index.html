help

Indian tax FAQ

Quick answers about privacy, data support, and how Tax Story helps Indian salaried taxpayers understand ITR and AIS data better.

Does Tax Story upload my ITR, AIS, or TIS files?

No. Parsing and analysis run locally in your browser by default. Your files stay on your device unless you export a PDF yourself.

Which tax files are supported?

Tax Story supports ITR JSON, legacy ITR XML, AIS JSON, and optional TIS PDF. You can upload a single year or many years without an artificial cap.

Can this help with old vs new regime decisions?

Yes. Tax Story compares old and new regimes using your extracted income and deductions, then estimates savings with FY-aware tax rules.

Is Tax Story a filing portal or legal advice?

No. It is an analysis and insight tool for taxpayers. Final filing decisions should be validated with the official portal and your tax advisor when needed.

menu_book How to get your portal exports

1
Login to incometax.gov.in

Go to e-File → Income Tax Returns → View Filed Returns. Click the year, then Download JSON for the ITR JSON.

2
AIS JSON (best for this tool)

Dashboard → Services → Annual Information Statement (AIS) → Proceed → AIS tile → Download → AIS-JSON. Note: some portals produce an encrypted utility file — use AIS PDF + password instead in that case.

3
TIS PDF (optional)

Same AIS portal page → Download → TIS PDF. Password: lowercase PAN + DOB as DDMMYYYY (example, not a real PAN: xxxxx0000xDDMMYYYY).

4
AIS PDF (alternative to JSON)

Same AIS portal → Download → AIS-PDF. Password same as TIS. Use when JSON is encrypted. Totals are approximate — prefer JSON.
